Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowed for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons also have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • At 5 months old, panda cubs learn how to climb - sometimes practicing by climbing on their mother
  • Pandas have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food
  • Unlike other bears, giant pandas do no hibernate during winter months, but instead may migrate short distances to lower elevations
